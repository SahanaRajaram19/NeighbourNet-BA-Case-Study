# 🏘️ NeighbourNet – Business Analyst Case Study

## 📖 Overview

NeighbourNet is a Business Analyst portfolio project that proposes a trusted hyperlocal community platform where residents can borrow, rent, sell, exchange, and donate household items while also discovering verified local service providers.

This project demonstrates the complete Business Analysis lifecycle, from identifying the business problem to proposing a solution, defining requirements, and measuring business success.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Residents currently depend on WhatsApp groups and word-of-mouth to borrow items or hire nearby service providers. These methods lack trust, verification, secure payments, and structured communication.

NeighbourNet aims to solve these challenges by creating a verified community platform.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Build trust among neighbours
- Encourage resource sharing
- Connect residents with verified local service providers
- Reduce unnecessary purchases
- Create a secure and transparent community marketplace

---
